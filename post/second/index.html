1.2.4.1 项目和开发生命周期 Project and Development Life Cycle

项目生命周期指项目从启动到完成所经历的一系列阶段。A project life cycle is the series of phases that a project passes through from its start to its completion.

项目生命周期可以是预测型或适应型。 Project life cycles can be predictive or adaptive.

Development life cycles can be predictive, iterative, incremental, adaptive, or a hybrid model:

  • 预测型Predictive Life Cycle:

    The project scope, time and cost are determined in the early phases of the life cycle.

Predictive life cycles may also be referred to as waterfall life cycles.

  • 迭代型 Iterative Life cycle:

    The project scope is generally determined early in the project life cycle, but time and cost estimates are routinely modified as the project team's understanding of the product increases.

  • 增量型 Incremental Life cycle:

    The deliverable is produced through a series of iterations that successively add functionality within a predetermined time frame.

  • 增量与迭代的区别：

    增量中间结果是可以交付的，整个项目多次交付； 迭代中间成果不交付，整个项目一次性交付，在项目过程中频繁地获取客户反馈

  • 适应型Adaptive life cycles

    Agile, iterative, or incremental.
适应性可以理解为更小的增量，客户频繁反馈并且在项目期间不断交付

  • 混合型Hybrid life cycle

       A combination of a predictive and an adaptive life cycle.
Project life cycles are independent of product life cycles, which may be produced by a project. A product life cycle is the series of phases that represent the evolution of a product, from concept through delivery, growth, maturity, and to retirement.

1.2.4.2 项目阶段 Project phase

一个或多个可交付成果的完成为结束

A project phase is a collection of logically related project activities thata culminates in the completion of one or more deliverables.

1.2.4.3 阶段关口 Phase gate

phase review, stage gate, kill point, phase entrance or exit

1.2.4.4 项目管理过程 Project Management Process

The project life cycle is managed by executing a series of project management activities know as project management processes.

一个过程输出成为以下二者之一：

  • 另一个过程输入 An input to another phase, or

  • 项目或项目阶段的可交付成果 A deliverable of the project or project phase.

过程通常分为三类：

  • 仅开展一次或仅在预定义点开展的过程 Processes used once or at predined points in the project.

  • 定期开展的过程 Processes that are performed periodically as needed.

  • 贯穿项目始终的过程。Processes that are performed continuously throughout the project.

1.2.4.5 项目管理过程组 Project Management Process Group

对管理过程进行逻辑分组 A logical grouping of project management processes.

  • Initiating Process Group
  • Planning Process Group
  • Executing Process Group
  • Monitoring and Controlling Process Group
  • Closing Process Group

1.2.4.7 项目管理数据和信息 Project Management Data and Information

  • 工作绩效数据 Work Performance data

​ The raw observation and measurement 原始观测和测量值，如项目完成百分比，缺陷数，变更数，实际成本，实际时间，活动实际进度

  • Work Performance information

整合分析得到的绩效信息，如交付成果的状态，变更请求的落实情况以及预测的完工尚需估算

  • Work Performance report

汇编工作绩效信息所形成的实物或电子项目文件。例如状况报告、备忘录、论证报告、信息札记、电子仪盘表、推荐意见和情况更新

Status Report, memos, justification report, information notes, electronic dashboards, recommandation , update.

1.2.5 裁剪Tailoring

方法论： 专业的从业人员采用的实践、技术、程序和规则所组成的体系

A methodology is a system of practices, techniques, procedures, and rules used by those who work in a discipline

Project management methodologies may be:

  • Developed by experts within the organization,

  • Purchased from vendors,

  • Obtained from professional associations, or

  • Acquired from government agencies.

Tailoring should address the competing constraints of scope, schedule, cost, quality , resource and risk. The project manager tailors the approach for managing these constraint based on project environmental, organizational culture, stakeholder needs and other variables.

1.2.6.1 商业论证

项目经理和发起人共同确保 项目章程、项目管理计划、效益管理计划在整个项目生命周期内始终保持一致。

内容 说明
目标收益 通过项目实施创造的有形价值和无形价值
战略一致性 项目收益和组织业务战略的一致程度
实现收益的时限 阶段、短期、长期和持续收益
收益责任人 已实现收益的负责人
测量指标 已实现收益的直接测量值和间接测量值
假设条件 预计存在或显而易见的因素
风险 实现预期收益的风险

2.4.2 管理要素

  • 基于专业技能和可用性开展工作的部门;
  • 组织授予的工作职权;
  • 工作职责，开展组织根据技能和经验等属性合理分派的工作任务;
  • 具有纪律性的行为(例如尊重职权、人员和规定);
  • 统一指挥原则(例如一位员工仅接受一个上级对任何行动或活动给出的指示);
  • 统一领导原则(例如针对一组活动只能有一个计划或一个领导人，以及相同的目标);
  • 组织的总体目标优先于个人目标;
  • 支付合理的薪酬;
  • 资源的优化使用;
  • 畅通的沟通渠道;
  • 在正确的时间让正确的人使用正确的材料做正确的事情;
  • 公正、平等地对待所有员工;
  • 明确工作岗位的安全职责;
  • 确保员工安全;
  • 允许任何员工参与计划和实施;
  • 保持员工士气。

在其影响力范围内担任多种角色，这些角色反映了项目经理的能力。

  • 项目 THE PROJECT

​ 1 实现项目目标，满足干系人期望，平衡制约项目成功的竞争因素

​ 2 好的沟通者

  • 组织 The Organization

    ​ 1 与其他项目经理互动，竞争：相同资源的需求; 资金分配优先顺序；可交付成果的接受和发布；目标一致性

    ​ 2 向组织展现项目管理的价值，提高组织对项目管理的接受度；提高组织内现有PMO的效率

  • 行业趋势 The Industry

    新产品新技术，变化的市场；标准；技术支持工具；影响当前项目的经济力量；影响项目管理学科；过程改进可持续发展战略

  • 专业学科 Professional Discipline

    ​ 1 对专业领域同行进行知识分享和知识传递;

  • 跨领域 Across Disciplines

​ 1 指导其他领域项目管理知识

​ 3.4.3 战略和商务管理 Strategic and business management

行业和组织的知识和专业技能 The knowledge of and expertise in the industry and organization that enhanced performance and better delivers business outcomes.

执行有利于战略调整(一致)和创新的决策和行动的能力 effectively negotiate and implement decisions and actions that support strategic alignment and innovation.

  • 向其他人解释关于项目的商业信息

  • 与项目发起人、团队和主题专家合作制定合适的项目交付策略

    Implement that strategy in a way that maximizes the business value of the project.
